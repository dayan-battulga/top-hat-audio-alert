# Privacy Policy for Top Hat Audio Alert

Effective date: April 23, 2026

Top Hat Audio Alert is a Chrome extension that plays a local audio alert when a new Top Hat question or participation prompt appears on supported Top Hat classroom pages.

## Information the extension accesses or stores

- Top Hat classroom page data: On `https://app.tophat.com/e/*`, the extension reads the current classroom page state needed to detect whether a new question or participation prompt has appeared. This local processing may include visible question or prompt titles and identifiers present in the page.
- Extension settings: The extension stores alert settings such as whether alerts are enabled, volume, selected sound, and popup theme in Chrome extension storage.
- Optional uploaded audio: If you choose to upload a custom MP3, the extension stores that audio file data locally in Chrome extension storage so it can be used for future alerts and previews.

## How the extension uses this information

The extension uses this information only to:

- detect new Top Hat questions or participation prompts on supported pages;
- play your selected alert sound or preview sound locally in the browser; and
- save your preferences and optional custom sound library on your device.

## Data sharing and transfer

Top Hat Audio Alert does not sell, rent, or share your data with the developer or third parties.

Top Hat Audio Alert does not send Top Hat page data, settings, or uploaded MP3 files to any remote server.

Top Hat Audio Alert does not use analytics, advertising, telemetry, or tracking services.

## Storage and retention

Data used by the extension is stored locally in Chrome extension storage on your device.

Optional custom MP3 uploads remain stored locally until you remove them, clear the extension's storage, or uninstall the extension.

The extension uses an offscreen document only to play audio locally inside the browser.

## Permissions used by the extension

- `https://app.tophat.com/e/*`: allows the extension to operate on supported Top Hat classroom pages.
- `storage`: allows the extension to save alert settings, theme preference, and optional custom MP3 data locally.
- `offscreen`: allows the extension to play alert audio in an offscreen document.

## Security

Because the extension does not transmit your data to the developer or third parties, Top Hat page data and optional uploaded MP3s remain local to your browser, subject to Chrome's extension storage mechanisms.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If it is updated, the effective date at the top of this page will also be updated.
